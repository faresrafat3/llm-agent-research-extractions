CoT research summary: Chain-of-thought prompting enables large language models to tackle complex arithmetic commonsense symbolic reasoning tasks. PaLM 540B with just eight chain-of-thought exemplars achieves SOTA on GSM8K. Properties: decompose multi-step problems, interpretable window, applicable any task humans can solve via language, readily elicited in sufficiently large off-the-shelf LLMs via few-shot prompting.
